About this game

Guess an integer from 'm' to 'n' (both included). Make your guess in as few attempts to score higher. Possible scores are integers from 1 to 1023 if your guess is successful, and 0 if you run out of attempts.

Limits

  • The difference between the two numbers must be less than or equal to (2 ^ 1023).
  • Minimum 1 attempt is required.
  • Do not enter numbers as powers, for example, 10 ^ 5 or 10 ** 5

Is this gambling?

Although this is a game of chance, we do not and will never ask for any money, bet or wager to play this game.
